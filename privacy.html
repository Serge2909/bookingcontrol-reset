Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Introduction

BookingControl ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and password (encrypted)
  • Booking Data: Guest names, phone numbers, dates, prices, and notes you enter
  • Property Information: Property names and configuration settings
  • Calendar Data: Booking information imported from external platforms (Airbnb, Booking.com, Ostrovok) via iCal URLs you provide
  • Preferences: Currency selection and app settings stored locally on your device
  • Usage Data: Device type, iOS version, and app version for support purposes

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the BookingControl service
  • Sync your data across your devices
  • Import bookings from external rental platforms via iCal integration
  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our app and develop new features

Third-Party Platform Integration

BookingControl allows you to connect external rental platforms (such as Airbnb, Booking.com, and Ostrovok) via iCal URLs.

When you provide an iCal URL:

  • We fetch calendar data directly from the external platform's servers
  • Imported booking data is stored in your account
  • We do not share your data with these platforms — the connection is read-only
  • You can remove iCal URLs at any time in the app settings

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud infrastructure provider. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption for data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication with automatic token refresh
  • Row-level security ensuring complete data isolation between users
  • Regular security audits and updates

Your preferences (such as currency selection) are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with:

  • Supabase: Our cloud infrastructure provider (for data storage and authentication)
  • Apple: For app distribution through the App Store
  • External rental platforms: Read-only access when you provide iCal URLs (Airbnb, Booking.com, Ostrovok, etc.)

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Remove third-party platform integrations at any time
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

BookingControl is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
