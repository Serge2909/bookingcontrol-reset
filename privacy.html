Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

Introduction

BookingControl ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the BookingControl mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • User ID (generated internally)

2. Booking and Property Data

  • Guest names
  • Guest phone numbers
  • Booking dates
  • Prices
  • Notes you enter
  • Property names and configuration settings

3. Calendar Integration Data

When you provide an iCal URL from external rental platforms (such as Airbnb, Booking.com, or Ostrovok):

  • We retrieve booking calendar data from the provided URL
  • Imported booking data is stored securely in your account
  • The integration is strictly read-only
  • We do not send your data to these platforms

You may remove iCal integrations at any time within the app.

4. Local Preferences

Certain preferences (such as selected currency and interface settings) are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the BookingControl service
  • Authenticate users
  • Enable core app functionality
  • Sync data across your devices
  • Import bookings via iCal integration
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure data security and account integrity

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing tracking, or analytics.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Supabase

We use Supabase as our secure cloud infrastructure provider for data storage and authentication. Supabase processes data on our behalf under strict security controls.

Apple

We share necessary information with Apple for app distribution through the App Store.

We do not share your data with advertising networks or third-party marketing services.

Data Storage and Security

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) to ensure complete data isolation between users
  • Access controls and secure server infrastructure

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Remove iCal integrations at any time
  • Contact us regarding data concerns

Children’s Privacy

BookingControl is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this document.
